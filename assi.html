simple omelette recipe

An easy and quick dish perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines baten eggs cooked to perfection optionally filled with your choice of cheese vegetables or meats.

preparation time
  • total: approximately
  • preparation:5 minutes
  • cooking :5 minutes

    • ingredients
  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt,to tast
  • pepper,to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter or oil
  • optional filling :cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats,herbs

    • instructions

  • Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  • Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffier texture.
  • Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs evenlyat the surface
  • Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  • Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully liff one edge and fold it over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto a plate
  • Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional sait and pepper if needed
